How It Works

Schedule a Pickup

Scheduling a pickup on GoScrap is a fast and easy process. You can schedule a pickup time according to your convenience.

Pickup at Your Address

On the day of the pickup, the GoScrap team of experienced professionals will visit your location. They'll assess the amount and type of scrap you have, and then load it onto their truck for transport to their facility for processing and recycling.

Receive Payment

Payment will be made on the spot after the materials have been weighed and inspected. In some cases, payment may be made by check or electronic transfer after the materials have been processed and sold.
